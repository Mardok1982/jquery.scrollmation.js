Scrollmation: A jQuery Plugin for scrolling animations

How To Use

Options

Scrollmation gives you quite a bit of flexibility to customize it's animations

Fade In

$('.fadein').scrollmation({'action': 'fadein'});

Fly Right

$('.flyright').scrollmation({'action': 'flyright'});
